The Philippines is a country that has everything a tourist could want, great culture, unique cuisine, and some of the most breathtaking islands and beaches in the world. As a fast-emerging destination hotspot in Southeast Asia, the Philippines is only becoming more and more popular among tourists looking for a new wonderland to discover. In 2019, the Philippines recorded over 8 million foreign tourists, and this number seems to only increase year after year. Each one of these tourists has their own reasons as to why they decided to visit the beautiful country, but here we explore 7 of the biggest reasons why tourists visit the Philippines.
